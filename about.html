Chih Chiu (b. 1990) lives and works in Taipei. He holds a Master’s degree in Innovation Design Engineering from Royal College of Art and Imperial College London. He previously worked as a design engineer at Volkswagen Group Future Center Asia and is currently a part-time lecturer at Shih Chien University.

Deeply influenced by his background in design and technology, Chih Chiu’s artistic practice integrates precision engineering, automated systems, and interactive elements to amplify subtle yet often overlooked sensory details in everyday experiences, to create new interpretations of the relationship between material and observer.

Chih has received the Next Art Tainan Award and a Jury Special Mention at the 17th Arte Laguna Prize in Venice. His solo exhibitions include "Preparation Before Sketching" (2023, Node94, Taipei), and he has participated in group exhibitions at the Bruges Triennial (Belgium), Natural History Museum Bern (Switzerland), Science Gallery Dublin (Ireland), and Liljevalchs Art Gallery (Sweden).

Additionally, his works have been featured in media outlets such as The Guardian and BBC News Chinese, and have been published in several books, including "Un art écologique: Création plasticienne et anthropocène." His kinetic installation at Taipei Songshan Cultural and Creative Park engaged with over 140,000 visitors through interactive participation.

邱馳，1990 年生，工作生活於台北。碩士畢業於英國皇家藝術學院與倫敦帝國學院的創新設計工程，曾於福斯汽車集團亞洲未來中心擔任設計工程師，現為實踐大學兼任講師。

他的藝術實踐深受設計與技術背景影響，擅長結合精密設計、自動系統與互動元素，放大日常經驗中的感知細節，讓物質與觀者的關係產生新的詮釋。

邱馳曾獲威尼斯 Arte Laguna Prize 評審團特別提名獎及台南新藝獎。他的個展包括《寫生前準備》（2023，台北 Node94），並參與比利時布魯日三年展、瑞士伯恩自然歷史博物館、愛爾蘭都柏林科學畫廊、瑞典 Liljevalchs 美術館等群展。

此外，他的作品曾受英國《衛報》、BBC News 中文等媒體報導，並收錄於多本出版物，包括 《生態藝術：人類世與造型的創作》。他在台北松山文創園區展出的動力裝置，吸引超過 14 萬名觀眾參與互動。
